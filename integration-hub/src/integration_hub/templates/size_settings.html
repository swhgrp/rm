How Size & Containers Work

Size Format: [Quantity] [Unit] [Container]

  • 1 L bottle - 1 liter bottle
  • 750 ml bottle - 750 milliliter bottle
  • 25 lb bag - 25 pound bag
  • 12 oz can - 12 ounce can

Conversion Factors:

  • Size units have conversion factors to a base unit (ml for volume, g for weight)
  • This enables price comparison across different sizes
  • Example: 1 L = 1000 ml, 1 lb = 453.592 g
