Calendar Sync Setup

iPhone / iPad Setup
  1. Open Settings on your iPhone/iPad
  2. Scroll down and tap Calendar
  3. Tap Accounts
  4. Tap Add Account
  5. Tap Other
  6. Tap Add CalDAV Account
  7. Enter these details:
    Server: rm.swhgrp.com
    Username: Your portal username (e.g., andy)
    Password: Your portal password
    Description: SW Events
  8. Tap Next to verify the connection
  9. Tap Save
Important: Use your portal username (e.g., andy), NOT your email address.
Android Setup

Android requires a third-party CalDAV app. We recommend DAVx5.

  1. Install DAVx5 from the Play Store
  2. Open DAVx5 and tap + to add account
  3. Select Login with URL and user name
  4. Enter these details:
    Base URL: https://rm.swhgrp.com/caldav/
    Username: Your portal username
    Password: Your portal password
  5. Tap Login
  6. Select the calendars you want to sync
What You'll See

Once connected, you'll have separate calendars for each venue:

  • The Links Grill
  • SW Grill
  • Seaside Grill
  • Park Bistro
  • Okee Grill
  • The Nest Eatery

Sync is one-way: Events from the web app sync to your phone. Changes made on your phone will NOT sync back to the Events system.

Troubleshooting
"Cannot Connect" or "Invalid Credentials"
  • Make sure you're using your username, not your email
  • Check that your password is correct
  • Ensure you have an active internet connection
Events Not Showing
  • Only events created/updated after CalDAV setup will appear
  • To sync older events, edit and save them in the Events system
  • Check that the venue calendars are enabled in your phone's calendar app
