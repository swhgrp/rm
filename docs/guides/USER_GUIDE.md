## 🌐 Getting Started

### How to Access the System

1. **Open your web browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
2. **Go to:** https://rm.swhgrp.com
3. **Choose your module:**
   - Click **"Inventory Management"** for inventory tasks
   - Click **"Accounting"** for accounting tasks

### First Time Login

When you're added to the system:

1. **Check your email** for a welcome message from admin@swhgrp.com
2. **Click the setup link** in the email
3. **Create your password** (must be at least 8 characters)
4. **Log in** with your username and new password

**Important:** The setup link expires in 24 hours. If it expires, contact your administrator.

### Logging In

1. Go to https://rm.swhgrp.com
2. Click on **Inventory Management** or **Accounting**
3. Enter your **username** and **password**
4. Click **Login**

### Forgot Your Password?

Contact your system administrator at admin@swhgrp.com to request a password reset link.

## 📦 Common Tasks

### Taking an Inventory Count

**What it does:** Record physical counts of items at your location

**Steps:**
1. Click **Inventory** → **Take Inventory**
2. Select a **template** (or create a new count)
3. Enter **quantities** for each item
4. Click **Save** to save progress (can continue later)
5. Click **Submit** when complete

**Tips:**
- ✅ Save frequently to avoid losing work
- ✅ Use templates for regular counts
- ✅ You can count in any order
- ✅ Mobile-friendly - use your tablet or phone

### Viewing Inventory Levels

**Steps:**
1. Click **Dashboard** or **Inventory** → **Current Levels**
2. Select your **location** from the dropdown
3. View current quantities and values
4. Use **search** to find specific items
5. Click **Filter** to show only certain categories

### Entering an Invoice

**What it does:** Record received deliveries and purchases

**Steps:**
1. Click **Purchasing** → **Invoices**
2. Click **Add Invoice**
3. Select **vendor**
4. Enter **invoice number** and **date**
5. Add **items** and **quantities**
6. Upload **photo of invoice** (optional but recommended)
7. Click **Save**

**Tips:**
- ✅ Take a photo of the paper invoice
- ✅ Double-check quantities before saving
- ✅ Invoice numbers must be unique

### Recording Waste

**What it does:** Track spoilage, breakage, or expired items

**Steps:**
1. Click **Inventory** → **Waste**
2. Click **Add Waste Record**
3. Select **item**
4. Enter **quantity** wasted
5. Select **reason** (spoilage, breakage, expired, etc.)
6. Add **notes** if needed
7. Click **Save**

### Recording a Transfer

**What it does:** Move inventory between locations or storage areas

**Steps:**
1. Click **Inventory** → **Transfers**
2. Click **Create Transfer**
3. Select **from location/area** and **to location/area**
4. Add **items** and **quantities**
5. Add **notes** if needed
6. Click **Submit**

**Note:** The person receiving the transfer should confirm receipt.

### Generating Reports

**Steps:**
1. Click **Reports**
2. Choose **report type:**
   - Inventory Valuation
   - Usage Report
   - Variance Report
   - Waste Report
   - etc.
3. Select **date range**
4. Select **location** (or "All Locations")
5. Click **Generate Report**
6. View on screen or click **Export to PDF/Excel**

**Staff Role:**
- ✅ Take inventory counts
- ✅ Record waste

- ✅ View reports

**Admin Role:**
- ✅ Full access to everything
- ✅ Manage users
- ✅ System settings
- ✅ POS configuration
- ✅ All reports
- **Access:** All locations

## ⚠️ Common Questions

### Why can't I see certain locations?

**Answer:** Your account is assigned to specific locations. If you need access to another location, contact your administrator.

### Why can't I edit an invoice?

**Answer:** Invoices can only be edited by Managers and Admins. If you're a Manager and still can't edit, the invoice might be finalized or locked.

### What does "Sync with POS" mean?

**Answer:** The system automatically imports sales data from your POS (point of sale) system every few hours. This helps track what's been sold and used.

### Can I delete a count after submitting it?

**Answer:** No, submitted counts cannot be deleted (for audit purposes). Contact your administrator if there's an error.

### Why do I see "No items found"?

**Answer:** Try these steps:
1. Clear your search/filter
2. Select the correct location
3. Check that items exist in that location
4. Refresh your browser (F5 or Ctrl+R)

### How do I upload photos?

**Answer:**
1. Click the **Upload** or **Choose File** button
2. Select photo from your device
3. Photo must be under 10MB
4. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, PDF

### Can I work offline?

**Answer:** No, you need an internet connection. However, if you lose connection while entering data, your browser may save some information temporarily.

## 💡 Pro Tips for Efficiency

### For Inventory Counts:
- ✅ Use **templates** - save time on regular counts
- ✅ Work **top to bottom** - systematic approach
- ✅ **Save frequently** - don't lose your work
- ✅ Use **search** - quickly find items
- ✅ **Round to nearest unit** - unless very expensive items

### For Invoice Entry:
- ✅ **Take photos** - capture paper invoices on your phone
- ✅ **Enter same day** - don't let them pile up
- ✅ **Check quantities** - verify before saving
- ✅ **Use vendor item codes** - faster matching

### For Daily Use:
- ✅ **Bookmark the site** - quick access
- ✅ **Keep logged in** - saves time (session lasts 30 min)
- ✅ **Use filters** - find what you need fast
- ✅ **Export reports** - save as PDF or Excel
- ✅ **Ask questions** - contact admin if unsure

## 🆘 Getting Help

### System Not Working?

**Try these steps:**
1. **Refresh the page** (F5 or Ctrl+R)
2. **Clear browser cache** and reload
3. **Log out and log back in**
4. **Try a different browser** (Chrome recommended)
5. **Contact administrator** if still having issues

### Contact Support

**Email:** admin@swhgrp.com
**Include in your message:**
- What you were trying to do
- What error message you saw (or screenshot)
- What browser you're using
- Your username

**Response Time:** Usually within 1 business day

- ✅ Use a **strong, unique password**
